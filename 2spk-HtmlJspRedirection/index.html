By default tomcat will take index.html
click here (href="dummyHTML") to redirect to another HTML.Redirected to the index.html present in the dummyHTML folder.
Note:
The href is dummyHTML and not /dummyHTML
/ will point to the root directory next to the webapps folder.So tomcat will search for another app inside webapps with the name dummyHTML when you use /dummyHTML.
For understanding click here and watch the URL

click here (href="dummyJSPHTML") to redirect to another folder having index.html and index.jsp

click here (href="dummyJSPHTML/myJSPpage.jsp") to redirect to another folder having myJSPpage.jsp
